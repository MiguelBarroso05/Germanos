What are cookies and what are they for?
Cookies are small files that websites place on your computer's hard drive when you first visit them or when they have been removed. Cookies are designed to be a reliable mechanism for websites to remember user activity information, such as saved passwords, items added to an online shopping cart, previously clicked links, among others. The website germanos.com uses session cookies, which are temporary and remain in the cookie file only until the user closes the browser. The information obtained by these cookies helps to analyze web traffic patterns, allowing us to identify problems and provide a better browsing experience.

Is it possible to disable the use of cookies?
Yes, cookies can be disabled directly in your browser. However, if you choose to disable cookies, some features of this website may not work properly. To block or delete cookies from this site, you can do so by changing your browser settings. Usually, this option is found in the "Preferences" or "Tools" menu. If you have trouble finding this option, refer to the "Help" menu of the browser you are using.
