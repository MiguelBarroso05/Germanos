WHO ARE WE?

Germano's is a company specializing in chauffeur-driven car rentals and travel agency services, with offices located in Lisbon, Porto, and Algarve. With a fleet of luxury Mercedes vehicles, we focus on leisure and business tourism, as well as high-level event organization.

We invite you to create a personalized itinerary with us, starting from major European cities heading to Portugal. We will suggest options tailored to your time and interests, presenting thematic, cultural, or recreational proposals, or simply guiding you to your desired destination.

We have experienced, certified drivers fluent in several languages, providing an exclusive service. You can choose between regular circuits or visit religious sites such as the famous Fátima Sanctuary in Portugal or Santiago de Compostela in Spain. Explore the wine route, visit stud farms with Lusitano horses, discover typical villages, or dive into the Jewish and Moorish heritage. We can also arrange visits to historic cities classified as UNESCO World Heritage Sites, guided by local experts. We also propose an exclusive culinary experience where you can prepare a Portuguese recipe under the guidance of a renowned chef.

Regarding executive services, we guarantee discretion, precision, and punctuality, with vehicles authorized to use exclusive public transport lanes (BUS), ensuring faster transfers. Allow us to offer our assistance and expertise during your stay, providing a unique experience where Portugal's culture, ancient history, and rich gastronomy come together with a touch of refinement and good taste.
